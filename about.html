About Bonkey Puzzles

Five puzzles. One hyperactive rabbit. Built with love.

What is Bonkey Puzzles?

Bonkey Puzzles is a family-friendly mobile puzzle collection bringing together five beloved classic game types — Crossword, Sudoku, Word Search, Word Ladder, and Code Words — in a single joyful app.

The app is designed for all ages. Kids can start on Easy and work their way up; adults can jump straight to Hard. With Daily Challenges, streak rewards, and hundreds of puzzles across two beautiful themes, there is always something new to explore.

Bonkey Puzzles is fully offline, contains no advertising, and makes no network calls during gameplay. All progress is stored locally on your device. It is safe for the whole family.

Meet the mascot

Say hi to Bonkey!

Bonkey is a hyperactive, always-smiling rabbit who loves space, summer, and solving puzzles at warp speed. He shows up on the splash screen as the hero, and appears as a cheerful sidekick during gameplay to celebrate your wins (and commiserate your losses).

Bonkey's outfit is a curious mix of tropical summer gear — Hawaiian shirt, sun hat, flip-flops — and space explorer accessories: a jetpack, space patches, and the occasional asteroid he bounces off for fun. Classic Bonkey.

Built with Godot Engine

Bonkey Puzzles is developed with the open-source Godot Engine (MIT License). We chose Godot for its lightweight footprint, active community, and first-class support for Android and iOS.

No subscriptions, ever

Every game comes with a free pack. Optional puzzle packs are one-time purchases through Google Play and the App Store — no recurring charges, no expiry dates.

Privacy first

We collect no personal data. No analytics SDK, no crash reporter, no advertising. All game data stays on your device. Read our full privacy policy.

Family friendly

Designed for players of all ages. Three difficulty levels, large tap targets, bright visuals, and a cheerful mascot make it approachable for young puzzlers and satisfying for seasoned ones.
