App descriptions

Short (80 chars)

"Five classic puzzles starring Bonkey — the hyperactive space-summer rabbit!"

Long (store listing)

Bonkey Puzzles is a family-friendly mobile puzzle collection bringing together five beloved classic game types — Crossword, Sudoku, Word Search, Word Ladder, and Code Words — in a single joyful app starring Bonkey, the hyperactive space-summer rabbit. Play offline anywhere, earn daily streak rewards, and choose from Sunshine or Cosmic Night themes. Built with the Godot Engine. No ads. No subscriptions. No data collected.

Meet Bonkey

Bonkey is a hyperactive, always-smiling rabbit dressed in a wild mix of tropical summer gear and space explorer accessories — Hawaiian shirt, sun hat, flip-flops, jetpack, and space patches.

He appears as the full-screen hero on the splash and home screens, and as an animated sidekick throughout gameplay, celebrating wins and commiserating losses.
