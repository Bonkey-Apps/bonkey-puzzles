Support

Frequently asked questions for Bonkey Puzzles

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the app, go to Settings → Restore Purchases and tap the button. Make sure you are signed into the same Google or Apple account you used when you originally bought the pack.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. Bonkey Puzzles is fully offline. Once installed, you can play anywhere — on a plane, underground, wherever — with no internet connection at all.

What's included for free?

Every game includes a free pack: 10 puzzles per difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard). You never need to buy anything to enjoy the app.

What does the paid pack include?

The Single Game Pack ($5.00) adds 50 more puzzles per difficulty for one game of your choice. The Bundle Pack ($7.50, coming soon) adds 100 more puzzles per difficulty. Both are one-time purchases — no subscription, no expiry.

How do I switch between Sunshine and Cosmic Night themes?

Open the app and go to Settings. Under "App theme", tap Sunshine or Cosmic Night. The whole app re-skins instantly.

What are Daily Challenges?

Every day a new puzzle is unlocked in the Daily tab. Complete it to earn bonus stars and maintain your streak. A streak reward chest appears every 7 days.

How does difficulty work?

Each puzzle type has three difficulty levels — Easy, Medium, and Hard — with progressively larger grids and more words or numbers. You choose the difficulty when you pick a puzzle type. Your default can be set in Settings.

I found a bug or want to suggest a feature.

We would love to hear from you! Visit the Report a Bug page to open a GitHub issue or send us an email at bonkey.apps@gmail.com.

Something else?

Email us at bonkey.apps@gmail.com and we will get back to you as soon as we can.
