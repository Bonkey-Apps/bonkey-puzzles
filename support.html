Support & FAQ

How do I restore my purchases?

If you reinstall the app or switch devices, open the app and go to Settings → Restore Purchases. As long as you are signed into the same Google account that made the original purchase, your packs will be restored automatically.

Do I need an internet connection to play?

No. Every game in Bonkey Puzzles works fully offline. Puzzles are generated on your device and all progress is saved locally. You only need an internet connection for the initial download and for making in-app purchases.

What's included in the free pack?

Every game includes a free pack of 10 puzzles per difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard) — that's 30 puzzles per game, at no cost. No account required; just download and play.

What are the paid options?

  • Single Pack ($5.00) — 50 more puzzles per difficulty level for one game. One-time purchase.
  • Bundle Pack ($7.50, coming soon) — 100 more puzzles per difficulty level for one game. Best value for puzzle lovers.

All purchases are one-time. There are no subscriptions and no ads.
