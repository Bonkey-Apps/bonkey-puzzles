How do I restore my purchases?

If you reinstall the app or switch devices, your puzzle packs can be restored through Google Play. Open the app, go to Settings → Restore Purchases and sign in with the Google account you used to buy them. Purchases are tied to your Google account, not your device.

Does the app work offline?

Yes — completely. All puzzles are stored on your device and play without any internet connection. The only time connectivity is needed is for in-app purchases.

What’s included for free?

Every game comes with a free pack of 10 puzzles per difficulty level — Easy, Medium, and Hard. You can play these at any time with no purchase required.

What do the paid packs include?

  • Single Pack ($5.00) — 50 additional puzzles per difficulty for one game. One-time purchase, no subscription.
  • Bundle Pack ($7.50, coming soon) — 100 additional puzzles per difficulty for one game. Best value per puzzle.

Which games are available?

Bonkey Puzzles includes five games: Sudoku, Word Search, Word Ladder, Crossword, and Codeword. Each game is independent and has its own puzzle packs.
