Bonkey Puzzles — Frequently Asked Questions

Is an internet connection required to play?

No. Bonkey Puzzles is fully offline. All puzzles are bundled with the app and no network connection is ever needed to play.

What’s included for free?

Every game includes a free pack with 10 puzzles per difficulty level—Easy, Medium, and Hard. No purchase or account required to access these.

What do the paid packs include?

Single Pack ($5.00): 50 additional puzzles per difficulty for one game. One-time purchase—no subscription.

Bundle Pack ($7.50, coming soon): 100 additional puzzles per difficulty for one game. Best value per puzzle.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases inside the app. As long as you are signed in to the same Google Play account used for the original purchase, your packs will be restored automatically.

Will I lose my progress if I reinstall the app?

Progress is saved locally on your device. Reinstalling the app will reset local progress unless your device has Android auto-backup enabled. Purchased packs are always restorable via Google Play.
