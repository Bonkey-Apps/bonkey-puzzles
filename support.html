Frequently asked questions for Bonkey Puzzles

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the app, go to Settings → Restore Purchases and tap the button. Make sure you're signed in to the same Google account you used when you originally bought the pack.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. Bonkey Puzzles is fully offline. Once installed, you can play anywhere — on a plane, underground, wherever — with no internet connection at all.

What's included for free?

Every game includes a free pack: 10 puzzles per difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard). You never need to buy anything to enjoy the app.

What does the paid pack include?

The Single Game Pack ($5.00) adds 50 more puzzles per difficulty for one game of your choice. The Bundle Pack ($7.50, coming soon) adds 100 more puzzles per difficulty. Both are one-time purchases — no subscription, no expiry.

I found a bug or want to suggest a feature.

We'd love to hear from you! Visit the Report a Bug page to submit an issue, or email us at bonkey.apps@gmail.com.

Something else?

Email us at bonkey.apps@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
