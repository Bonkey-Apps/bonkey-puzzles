Privacy Policy

Bonkey Puzzles — Last updated: June 24, 2026

Overview

Bonkey Puzzles is designed with privacy as a first principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All gameplay data stays on your device.

Data we collect

None. Bonkey Puzzles does not collect any personal data. There are no analytics SDKs, ad networks, crash reporters, or tracking tools in the app.

Local storage

The app saves your puzzle progress and settings locally on your device using Android’s standard storage APIs. This data:

  • Never leaves your device.
  • Is not backed up to any cloud service by the app (standard Android auto-backup may apply per your device settings).
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

Network access

The app makes no network calls during gameplay. An internet connection is never required to play. Puzzles and content are fully bundled with the app.

In-app purchases

Optional puzzle packs are available for purchase through Google Play Billing. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google. We never see or store your payment information. For details on how Google handles purchase data, see the Google Privacy Policy.

Children’s privacy

Bonkey Puzzles does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Because the app collects no data at all, it is safe for users of all ages.

Third-party services

The app does not integrate any third-party SDKs that collect data. No advertisers, analytics providers, or social platforms receive any information through this app.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated “Last updated” date. We will never introduce data collection without a clear notice and policy update.

Contact

Questions or concerns about privacy? Contact us at bonkey.apps@gmail.com.
