Privacy Policy

← Bonkey Puzzles

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Bonkey Puzzles is published by Bonkey Apps. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

No personal data collected

Bonkey Puzzles does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not ask for your name, email address, location, or any other identifying information.

Local-only storage

All game progress and settings are saved locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

  • Puzzle progress and completion records
  • In-app settings and preferences
  • Unlocked puzzle packs

No network calls during gameplay

The app is fully offline. No data is sent over the internet during gameplay. An internet connection is only used for in-app purchases processed by Google Play.

In-app purchases

Optional puzzle packs are available for purchase through Google Play Billing. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google. We do not receive, store, or process your payment information. Please review Google’s Privacy Policy for details on how Google handles purchase data.

No advertising or analytics

Bonkey Puzzles contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, and no crash reporters that transmit data off your device.

Children

Bonkey Puzzles does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. Because we collect no data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at bonkey.apps@gmail.com.
