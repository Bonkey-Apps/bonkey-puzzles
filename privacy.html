Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

Bonkey Puzzles is developed and published by Bonkey Apps. This policy describes how the app handles your data — in short, it barely handles any at all.

Data we collect

We collect no personal data. The app does not ask for your name, email address, location, or any other identifying information.

Local storage

Bonkey Puzzles saves your progress and settings locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device.
  • Is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.
  • Can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in Android settings.

Network access

Bonkey Puzzles does not make any network requests during gameplay. All puzzles are generated on-device and all saves are stored locally. An internet connection is never required to play.

Analytics and advertising

The app contains no analytics SDK, no advertising SDK, and no crash-reporting service that transmits data off-device.

In-app purchases

Optional puzzle packs can be purchased through Google Play Billing. Any payment or billing data you provide is handled entirely by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Children

Bonkey Puzzles does not knowingly collect any data from children or from anyone. Because no personal data is collected by the app, it is safe for all ages.

Third-party services

Beyond Google Play Billing (described above), the app uses no third-party services that collect or process personal data.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change our data practices, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will also be noted in the app's release notes.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at bonkey.apps@gmail.com.
